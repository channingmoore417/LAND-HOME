about this home

A bright, open layout in South Lake Charles

Set on a quiet, established street in desirable South Lake Charles, this four-bedroom home pairs a flexible open layout with finishes that make day-to-day living feel effortless. A wide foyer opens to a vaulted family room anchored by a gas fireplace and custom built-ins.

The kitchen was built for gathering — quartz counters, a center island with seating, walk-in pantry, and a sunlit breakfast nook that looks out over the covered patio. The primary suite sits privately apart, with dual vanities, a soaking tub, separate shower, and a generous walk-in closet.

Located in flood zone X — no flood insurance required. Zoned for Prien Lake schools. Two-car garage, fully fenced backyard, and a covered patio ready for Louisiana evenings.
